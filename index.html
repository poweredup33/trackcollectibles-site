Questions

№ 007 — THE FINE PRINT
When does it launch?

TrackCollectibles is in active development, targeting a mid-2026 iOS release. Join the waitlist above and you'll be the first to know — waitlist members get priority access to early TestFlight builds.

What can I scan?

Comics, trading cards, coins, vinyl records, toys & figures, jewelry, memorabilia, and more. The AI identifies items from a photo and pulls live market pricing automatically. Graded slabs (CGC, CBCS, PGX, and more) are fully supported.

Does it sell items for me?

In V1, the app generates a complete, ready-to-paste eBay listing that you review and post yourself — no auto-listing. Direct in-app selling and trading are on the V2 roadmap.

Do my items need to be graded or slabbed?

No. TrackCollectibles works with raw, ungraded items just as well as slabbed ones. For graded slabs it reads the label directly; for raw items it identifies them visually and cross-references databases, then prices them from comparable live eBay listings. Grading is never required.

How accurate is the AI?

Very accurate on text-rich items like graded slabs (reads the label directly). For raw items it uses visual AI plus cross-referenced database lookups. You always review and confirm the identification before saving — nothing gets added to your collection without your approval.

Is my collection data private?

Yes. Scan photos are processed and discarded — they're not stored permanently. Your collection data is yours, stored securely in your account. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What is the Lifetime Founder plan?

A one-time $129 payment that gives you unlimited collection slots and 100 AI listings per month — for life. It's strictly limited and will never be reopened once sold out. Early waitlist members get first access when the app launches.
